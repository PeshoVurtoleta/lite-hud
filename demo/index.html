lite-hud 2.0.0 -- SPP-native zero-GC overlay

A simulated game loop pushes fps, frame time, gc events, and draw counts through the HUD's SPP demux. The overlay renders at ~15 Hz from preallocated ring buffers -- zero allocation on every channel.push(). Frame time is wired through a mock @zakkster/lite-scope with a 60fps budget line; the rest are manual channels. Press ` to toggle the overlay, click a channel label in the HUD to hide/show it.
